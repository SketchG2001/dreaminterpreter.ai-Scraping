    Symbol: Qandeel Meaning: The presence of qandeel in a dream symbolizes self-expression, empowerment, and breaking societal norms. This dream may serve as a reminder to reconnect with one's authentic self and to express one's unique qualities without hesitation or fear. Overall, dreams involving qandeels symbolize the need for self-expression, the importance of breaking free from societal norms, and the desire to live life authentically. These dreams encourage individuals to embrace their true selves and follow their own path, even if it means challenging the expectations of others.
